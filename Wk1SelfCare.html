My sanity saver this year was my spin bike. I am far too cheap to buy a Peloton and then wait 8 weeks for it to arrive. For 1/2 the price of one Peloton bike, we purchased two Sunny Evolution Pro II bikes. Same magnetic resistance and sturdy feel, but it arrived within a week and allowed my husband and I to workout together.

We tried the Peloton app by streaming it through our Amazon Firestick and were underwhelmed. The Les Mills On Demand app offered more engaging classes like:

  • The Trip
  • Sprint
  • RPM
  • and many classes that do not require a spin bike like Body Pump
